Park Trifecta

After analysis of the community around West Side Park in Newark New Jersey, a set of programs including student work spaces, a cafe, bookstore, library, multipurpose space and exhibition space were introduced to the site. I wanted to address the existing staircase “shelf” condition of the landscape in an additive and transformative way. Inserting volumes into the shelf-like space and pushing the stairs into those volumes with glazed facades resulted in a transparent relationship with the community.
